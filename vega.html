Vega — Condition Monitoring Solutions

VK Controls — Channel Partner

VEGA is a leading manufacturer of level and pressure measurement instrumentation for process industries worldwide. As a channel partner, VK Controls offers VEGA's complete range of condition monitoring and measurement solutions, backed by expert installation and after-sales support.

Our team helps you select, install and commission the right VEGA instruments for your specific process requirements — ensuring accuracy, reliability and long service life.

Solutions We Offer

  • Level measurement — radar, guided wave radar & ultrasonic sensors
  • Pressure measurement instruments
  • Point level detection & switching devices
  • Condition monitoring for rotating equipment
  • Installation, commissioning & calibration support
